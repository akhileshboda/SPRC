2. Description of Service

Kindred is a coordinator-managed resource connection platform that helps individuals with special needs access community events, vocational programs, and employment opportunities. The platform is not a general public service — participation is facilitated by trained coordinators who manage onboarding and account creation.

Kindred provides:

  • A public events and jobs board viewable without an account.
  • A registration pathway for prospective participants and volunteers to express interest.

  • Communication tools including newsletters, notifications, and work queue management for coordinators.

Kindred does not guarantee placement in any program, event, or job opportunity. All listings are published at the discretion of coordinators.
