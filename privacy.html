Privacy & data use

How Kindred handles information in this project deployment.

Last updated: April 2026. Replace this page with your organization’s full privacy policy and legal review before production use.

What this site stores

Kindred may store account details, profile information, event and job listings, and workflow data needed to run the platform (e.g. interests, approvals, and messages tied to a coordinator-managed account).

Who can see your data

Data is limited by role (participant, guardian, volunteer, or administrator). Coordinators and administrators manage accounts; families and participants use the portal for agreed purposes only.

Local demo / term project

Some deployments use browser storage for demonstration. A production system should use secure servers, encryption in transit, retention policies, and a published notice that matches your jurisdiction.
